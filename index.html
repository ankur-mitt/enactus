Our current ventures

Project SHWAS

With a motto of “Let Punjab breathe a safe life”, the project SHWAS aims to provide natural, fresh air to the people of Northern India.
In addition to reducing the pollution from stubble burning, this project emphasizes on putting stubble into eco-friendly and productive uses.
The project directly impacts local inhabitants, farmers and cattle raisers, biogas plant owners, and industries utilizing stubble for productive purposes. The people impacted indirectly are residents of Delhi and other parts of Northern India.

Project S-TREND

An estimated USD 500 billion value is lost every year due to the people who buy several clothes and rarely wear all of them and later discard them.
Strend ensures that clothes which are stocked up in cupboards serving no purpose go in the hands of those who really need and value them. It benefits someone at each step.
Till now Strend has impacted over 3,00,000 people indirectly and 600+ people directly with areas including Ropar and nearby villages, Chandigarh and Kullu.
